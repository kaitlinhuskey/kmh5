Volleyball 101

Here is everything you need to know about volleyball!

Volleyball is a sport for both men and women, that has a maximum of six players playing at a time.

    History:

  • Originally called mintonette, it was created by: William G. Morgan in 1895
  • He designed the game to be a combination of basketball, baseball, tennis and handball
  • USVBA (United States Volleyball Association) was formed in 1928, since it became clear that rules needed to be enforced for tournament play
  • In 1900, there was a special ball designed for the sport
  • Set and spike were introduced in the Philippines in 1916
  • In 1920, the rule for three touches per side and back row attack rules were instituted


      • Here are the basic rules:

      1. 6 players on the court at all times: 3 in front and 3 in back
      2. Maximum of 3 touches per side
      3. Rally-point scoring: points are made on every serve
      4. A player can't touch the ball twice in a row
      5. If the ball hits the boundary line or inside, the ball is in, but if the ball hits outside the boundary line, it is out
      6. If the ball hits the antennae it is considered out of bounds
