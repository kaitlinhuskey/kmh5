Offense: Attacking

Here is everything you need to know about volleyball!

Volleyball is a sport for both men and women, that has a maximum of six players playing at a time.

    How it's done:

  • Attacking is a form of offense that is done at the net towards the other side of the net to the other team


      • The basics:

      1. An approach is made to the net, giving the player momentum to jump and hit the ball with highest potential
      2. The best way to hit the ball is when it is at its peak height from the setter's hands
      3. After leaving the hitter's hand, if done correctly, the ball should go down and either hit the court in-bounds or dug up from the other team
      4. Most times, after an attack is hit, it ends the rally when the defenders can't keep the ball in play
