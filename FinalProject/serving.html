Serving

Here is everything you need to know about volleyball!

Volleyball is a sport for both men and women, that has a maximum of six players playing at a time.

    Starting a rally:

  • Serving is how a rally is started in volleyball, and the first chance to get a point


      • The basics:

      1. It is done when a rally ends, after a point is given, and when a new rally is starting, the team that earned the point gets to serve
      2. The players rotate serving each rally and serve behind the out-of-bounds line
      3. The way to serve is shown above: tossing the ball up, taking a step or lunging forward and rotating the whole body to hit the ball with full force to get it over the net
      4. Another way to serve is to jump serve, and tossing the ball up higher than a standing serve, and doing an attacking approach to hit the ball as high as possible to get a fast (hopefully hard to return) serve to the opponent
